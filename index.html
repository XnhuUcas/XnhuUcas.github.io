About Me

 WELCOME to my page! I was born in Jinan, Shandong, on May 15th, 1992. I'm now a data scientist in Platform and Content Group(PCG), Tencent Co., Ltd. at Beijing. My main work focus on the data analysis of recommendation systems for Tencent Video, such as the user growth, user experience improvement, recommendation process improvement, and A/B testing.
