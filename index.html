Biography

I am currently a lecturer at Capital Normal University.. Prior to joining the CNU, I worked at Tencent as a data scientist from 2019 to 2021 and worked at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences as a postdoctoral fellow from 2021 to 2023. I received my PhD degree in Probability and Mathematical Statistics from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2019, and jointly trained at Yale University from 2017 to 2018.

Research Interests

  • Statistical machine learning: model averaging/ensemble, model selection evaluation, transfer learning.

  • Biostatistics: genome-wide association study, integrative analysis, summary statistics.

  • Statistical analysis of complex data: cancer/multi-omics data analysis, multi-source heterogeneous data analysis, Multi-modal data fusion.
